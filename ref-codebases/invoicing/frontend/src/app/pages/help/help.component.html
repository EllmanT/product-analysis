Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No, you can use our invoice generator as a guest without creating an account. However, registering gives you access to additional features like invoice history, fiscalization, product management, and draft saving.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Guest invoices are stored only in your browser's local storage. Registered user data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers with industry-standard security measures.

Can I customize my invoices?

Absolutely! You can add your business logo, customize colors, add notes and terms, include discounts and shipping costs, and adjust all invoice details to match your needs.

What is fiscalization?

Fiscalization is the process of registering your invoice with tax authorities for compliance purposes. This feature is available to registered users and ensures your invoices meet legal requirements in your jurisdiction.

Can I edit an invoice after creating it?

Guest users can edit their invoice before downloading. Registered users can save drafts and edit them until they're fiscalized. Once an invoice is fiscalized, it cannot be edited for legal compliance reasons.

What payment methods can I specify?

You can specify various payment methods including cash, bank transfer, credit card, or other payment options. Include payment instructions in the invoice notes section.
